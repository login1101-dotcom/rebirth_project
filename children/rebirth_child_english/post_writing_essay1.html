My First English Essay: Why I Code

2025.12.25 â€¢ Writing (English)

It is never too late to learn something new. Today, I want to write about my passion for coding and why I decided to improve my English skills at the age of 51.

The Magic of Creation

I love programming because it is like magic. You write lines of text, and suddenly, a computer does exactly what you want it to do. It feels like building a house, but without any physical materials.

When I was young, I didn't have many chances to learn about computers. But now, with the internet, I can learn anything. Building this "Re:Birth Project" is my way of proving that I can still grow.

English: The Global Key

Most modern programming documentation is written in English. To become a better developer, English is not just an option; it is a necessity.

However, reading documentation is one thing. Expressing my own thoughts is another challenge. That is why I started this "English Gym". I want to be able to discuss technology with people from all over the world.

Conclusion

Writing this essay took me about 30 minutes. It was hard to find the right words, but I feel accomplished. I will continue to write here to track my progress.

Thank you for reading. Let's keep learning!
